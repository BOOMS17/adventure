The Great Heist

You and a friend are robbing a bank holding billions of illegally obtained dollars. If you succeed, the bank won't report the heist to the police since the money was illegally obtained. Will you go through with the heist?

Your much dumber friend goes through with the plan despite your relucatance and throws a rock through the window. He is killed by the automatic laser machine gun security system that you could've disabled. Your guilt lasts until death.

The bank uses their money to fund genocides in third world countries. You could have prevented this. You lose.

Your friend wants to throw a rock through a window and run in to take the money. You want to use your awesome hacker skills to disable the security system. Will you convince your friend to go with your plan?

You rob the evil bank after disabling it's security system. You and your friend are rich for the rest of your lives. You win.
